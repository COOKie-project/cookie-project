What does COOKie do?

The idea behind the project

COOKie is your smart kitchen assistant. Helping you cook better, healthier, and without waste.

⏰
Expiry Reminders

Reminds you to use ingredients before they expire, reducing waste to zero.

🥗
Personalised Recipes

Recommends delicious and healthy recipes based on what's already in your fridge.

👨‍🍳
Cooking Assistance

Guides you step by step: timers, clear instructions, all in one place.

📱
Intuitive App

Simple and pleasant interface, designed for everyday use at home.

Why we exist

Our Mission

COOKie helps people cook more easily and intelligently at home, offering organisation, guidance and control over ingredients, recipes and time in the kitchen.

Our Vision

COOKie becomes the go-to digital sous-chef in every kitchen, turning daily cooking from stress into pleasure and food waste into the exception, not the rule.

Who we're for

Target Audience

Young people and adults interested in reducing food waste and living a healthier lifestyle. People who cook at home and want more control, more flavour and less stress in the kitchen.

of people end up throwing away groceries because they go bad.

🤖 The Robot Prototype

Meet the COOKie Robot

Our physical prototype is a compact smart device designed to sit on your kitchen counter. Equipped with a camera, it scans ingredients and communicates with the app in real time.

  • 📷 Built-in camera for ingredient scanning
  • 🔵 Teal design matching the COOKie brand
  • 🔗 Syncs with the mobile app
  • 🏠 Compact for any kitchen counter
